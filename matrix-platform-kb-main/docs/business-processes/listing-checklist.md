## Property Data Submission Requirements

> Source: `current busienss practice/Check-list Listing Residential Properties and Land ENG 2025-2026.xlsx`

### Submission Process
1. Email all listing data to: `listing@cyprus-sothebysrealty.com`
2. Include: filled checklist (approved by seller) + signed agreement + photo link + description
3. Photos: horizontal, >1MB, JPG/JPEG format
4. Photo ordering: rename with numbers (1, 2, 3... up to 30 max)
5. Description: 130-190 words, must comply with checklist data
6. Changes after website publication require a new update cycle

### Required Document Packages

**Properties WITH Title Deed:**
- Copy of Title Deed (verify it reflects the property correctly)
- Plans
- If Title Deed says only "land" → request Building Permit with approved plans + Certificate of Final Approval

**Properties WITHOUT Title Deed:**
- Copy of Contract of Sale with ALL attachments
- Copy of Title Deed to the land
- Copy of Building Permit with approved plans + Certificate of Final Approval
- Plans

### Viewing Checklist Data
- Key holder name and contact
- Contact person
- Notice requirements
