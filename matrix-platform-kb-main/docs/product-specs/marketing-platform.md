# Marketing Platform — Product Specification

> **For Lovable**: This spec defines campaign management and marketing automation.
> RESO Resources: Property, Media, Contacts, Member

---

## Overview

Campaign creation, audience segmentation, email/portal/social distribution, and analytics. Marketing Director manages campaigns across 3 markets with ROI visibility.

## User Persona

**Marketing Director**: Manages campaigns across Cyprus, Hungary, Kazakhstan. Needs ROI attribution, audience insights, asset reuse from CDL.

## Key User Flows

1. **Select Listings**: Choose listings for campaign → auto-pull media from CDL
2. **Segment Audience**: By market, budget, property type, engagement level
3. **Generate Assets**: Email templates, portal listing blocks, social posts
4. **Distribute & Track**: Send → track opens/clicks/inquiries → ROI report
